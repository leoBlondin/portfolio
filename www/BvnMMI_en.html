Presentation

Welcome to the MMI, is a presentation application of the MMI department of Montbéliard. In collaboration with my friend and colleague Anaël Rodet, we developed this mobile application using ionic. We have chosen to present the tools that the students will use. As well as useful and interesting resources for their integration into the work of the department. The application is simple and practical, based on the graphic charter of the department website.

What I learned

During the integration of the application, I used HTML and CSS to produce the design. During this project, I also worked on ionic/cordova. These two technologies are mainly used for navigation and page routing.
