Presentation

Montreal this week is a weekly magazine about tourism in Montreal. The objective was to implement page this periodical on the theme of our choice. The theme that I choose is the Techno music., Firstly because there is lots of musicals varieties in Montreal and it's my favorite music style. Some constraints were to choose only two different colours and two different typographies for all the magazine.

What I learned

During this exercise, I learned how to use Adobe InDesign to make the print of the magazine. Moreover, I learned how to calibrate colors and typographies concordance on a project.
