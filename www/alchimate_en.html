Alchimate is a hub on which you will be able to find the perfect team mates to play with you! This project was an exercise from the MMI departement of Montbéliard.

This is the landing page for the hub. You will be able to find informations about the platform, its functionalities and about the team creation.

What I learned

On this project, I was in charge of the development of the landing page. During the development, for example I discovered how to use Display:flex, who became the most important tool for my future works. I also worked with Javascript and Animate.CSS to embellish and animate this wonderful site! I was also in charge of the logo creation. It was a step made up of many tests. But we have found common ground!
